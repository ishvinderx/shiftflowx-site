Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

Is ShiftFlow really free to try?
Yes — every new account gets a full 30-day free trial with all features unlocked. No credit card required to start. You'll only be charged if you choose to subscribe after the trial ends.
What types of workers use ShiftFlow?
ShiftFlow is built for anyone who earns variable income: hourly employees, shift workers, freelancers, gig workers (Uber, DoorDash, Amazon Flex), independent contractors, and small business owners.
How does payday prediction work?
Our AI analyses your pay schedule history, past paychecks, shift patterns, and employer deposit behaviour to predict with high confidence exactly when your next payment will arrive — including the date and estimated amount.
Can ShiftFlow catch payroll mistakes?
Yes. We compare your expected pay (calculated from your logged hours and pay rate) against your actual paycheck. Any discrepancy — missing overtime, incorrect deductions, short hours — is flagged automatically so you can act on it immediately.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is end-to-end encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on secure servers, and is never sold to third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
Does ShiftFlow work outside the US?
Yes — ShiftFlow supports workers in the UK, Canada, Australia, India, South Africa, and more. The app handles multiple currencies and local tax frameworks so you get accurate estimates wherever you are.
