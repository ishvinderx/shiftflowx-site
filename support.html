Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad. Tap ShiftFlow and choose Cancel Subscription. You will retain access until the end of the current billing period. ShiftFlow cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf — Apple manages all billing.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

In the ShiftFlow app, go to Profile → Subscription and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID you used when you first subscribed.

My shifts are not syncing. What should I do?

Pull down to refresh on the Shifts screen. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in. Your data is securely stored on our servers and will be restored after sign-in.

How do I delete my account and all my data?

Go to Profile → Privacy → Delete Account in the app, or visit shiftflowx.net/delete for instructions. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Is my payslip data stored on your servers?

No. Payslip images are processed in-memory for OCR analysis and are not retained on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What currencies and countries are supported?

ShiftFlow supports 50+ currencies. Tax estimates are calculated for the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, and Australia. More countries are coming soon.

I found a bug or want to request a feature.

Email support@shiftflowx.net with the subject line Bug Report or Feature Request. Screenshots and device model are very helpful.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — ShiftFlow Pro includes a 30-day free trial for new users. No payment is required until the trial ends, and you can cancel at any time.

I was charged unexpectedly.

All charges are processed by Apple. If you believe you were charged in error, contact reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund. You can also email us at support@shiftflowx.net and we will help investigate.
