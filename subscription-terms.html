These Subscription Terms govern your purchase of ShiftFlow Plus ("the Subscription"), a paid tier that unlocks premium features in the ShiftFlow iOS application. By subscribing, you agree to these terms.

1. Plans and Pricing

  • ShiftFlow Plus Monthly — billed monthly. Price displayed in the App Store at the time of purchase.
  • ShiftFlow Plus Annual — billed annually. Offers a discounted effective monthly rate compared to the monthly plan.

Prices are displayed in your local currency in the App Store. Applicable taxes may be added by Apple depending on your location.

2. Free Trial

New users may be offered a free trial of ShiftFlow Plus. The trial duration is displayed before purchase. Your trial starts on the date you first use the app — reinstalling the app or creating a new account does not reset the trial. At the end of the trial, your selected plan begins billing automatically unless you cancel before the trial expires.

3. Billing and Renewal

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and renew automatically unless cancelled. Renewal charges are applied within 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time via the App Store → Subscriptions.

4. Cancellation

You may cancel your Subscription at any time through:

  • App Store → Your Apple ID → Subscriptions → ShiftFlow Plus → Cancel
  • Settings → Manage Subscription in the ShiftFlow app

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access to premium features until that date. No partial refunds are issued for unused days.

5. Refunds

All refund requests are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. ShiftFlow has no control over refund decisions made by Apple.

6. Price Changes

We reserve the right to change subscription prices. You will be notified of price changes through the App Store, and you will be asked to confirm the new price before your next billing cycle. Continuing to use the Subscription after a price change constitutes acceptance.

7. Premium Features

ShiftFlow Plus includes access to: Burnout Analytics, AI Recommendations, Payslip Scanner (OCR), Paycheck Verifier, Tax Centre, Invoice Generator, Shift Optimizer, Work Journal, Energy Tracker, Career Intelligence, Streak Tracking, Advanced Insights, and other features marked as Plus in the app. Feature availability may change over time.
