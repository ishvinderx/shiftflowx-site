Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 27, 2026

ShiftFlow ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, and authentication tokens when you register or sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Shift and earnings data — hours worked, clock-in/out times, pay rates, overtime, tips, mileage, and notes that you log in the app.
  • Payslip images — photos you upload for OCR analysis are processed in-memory and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • AI conversation messages — messages sent to the AI assistant are processed to generate replies and improve response quality.
  • Device and usage data — anonymized analytics (events, screen views, crash reports) to help us improve the app.
  • Payment and subscription status — managed via Apple's StoreKit and RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and sync your shift tracking, earnings reports, invoices, and tax estimates across your devices.
  • Calculate pay accuracy, burnout scores, and personalized recommendations.
  • Send push notifications you opt into (pay alerts, burnout nudges, weekly summaries).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Improve ShiftFlow features and fix bugs using aggregated, anonymized analytics.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data. Ever.
  • We share data only with service providers necessary to operate ShiftFlow: Railway (hosting), Supabase (database), RevenueCat (subscriptions), PostHog (analytics), and Anthropic (AI).
  • All service providers are bound by data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is soft-deleted immediately and permanently purged within 30 days. You may also export your data at any time from Settings → Export.

5. Security

All data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256. Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain and never in plaintext. We regularly audit our infrastructure for security vulnerabilities.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate information in the app.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data via Settings or at shiftflowx.net/delete.
  • Portability — export your shift data as CSV or PDF at any time.
  • Opt-out — disable analytics tracking in Settings → Privacy at any time.

7. Children

ShiftFlow is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or in-app notice. Continued use of ShiftFlow after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@shiftflowx.net
