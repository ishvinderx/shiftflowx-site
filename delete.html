Delete My Account

This page explains how to delete your ShiftFlow account and all associated data.

You can delete your ShiftFlow account directly from the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent — your data will be queued for purge within 30 days in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. All shift data, earnings history, invoices, goals, and AI memory will be permanently deleted. Export your data first if you want to keep a copy.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the ShiftFlow app on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the More tab (bottom right).
  3. Tap your profile name at the top.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by entering your email address when prompted.
  6. Tap Delete Permanently.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All shift and time entry records
  • Earnings and tax data
  • Invoices and financial records
  • Goals, budgets, and savings targets
  • AI chat history and AI memory
  • Work journal entries and energy profiles
  • All other personal data associated with your account

What Is Not Deleted Immediately

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked back to you.
  • Audit logs retained for legal/compliance purposes (purged after 90 days).
  • Backup copies (purged within 30 days).

Cancel Your Subscription First

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your App Store subscription. To avoid future charges, cancel your ShiftFlow Plus subscription separately via the App Store → Subscriptions before deleting your account.

Need Help?

If you are unable to delete your account from within the app, email us at privacy@shiftflowx.net and we will process the deletion manually within 5 business days.
