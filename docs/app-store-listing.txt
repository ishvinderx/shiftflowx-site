Tired of wondering when your next paycheck hits — or discovering it was short after the fact?

ShiftFlow is the financial command center built for shift workers, freelancers, and gig workers. Log your shifts, scan your paychecks for errors, and let our AI predict exactly when money lands in your account.

• AI Payday Prediction — Know your next payday before your employer confirms it. Our confidence-scored payday prediction engine learns your pay cycle and alerts you in advance so you're never caught off guard.

• Payroll Protection — The average worker loses $2,000+ a year to payroll errors. ShiftFlow's paycheck scanner automatically flags missing overtime, incorrect deductions, and underpayments before you cash the check.

• Shift Tracker & Work Hours Tracker — Log every shift in seconds. Track regular hours, overtime, and split shifts across multiple jobs. The built-in overtime calculator ensures you're paid every dollar you've earned.

• Income Tracker for Every Work Style — Whether you're hourly, salaried, freelance, or juggling gig work on Uber, DoorDash, or Amazon Flex, ShiftFlow consolidates all your income in one place.

• Tax Estimator — Avoid surprise tax bills. ShiftFlow estimates your quarterly and annual tax liability in real time so you can set aside the right amount each pay period.

• Invoice Generator — Freelancers and contractors can create and send professional invoices directly from the app. No spreadsheet juggling required.

• Burnout Tracker & Daily Check-In — Overwork is expensive. ShiftFlow's mood and stress check-in detects burnout patterns before they affect your health or income, and sends gentle nudges when you need a break.

• Financial Health Score — Your ShiftFlow Score gives you a single number that reflects your income stability, debt risk, and financial momentum — updated every week.

• Reports Hub — Beautiful earnings analytics, shift summaries, and income history reports you can export in one tap.

• Financial Coach — Personalized, AI-powered insights based on your actual income data. Not generic advice — real guidance tailored to your schedule and earnings.

WHO IT'S FOR
ShiftFlow is built for nurses and healthcare workers pulling long shifts, retail and warehouse employees managing unpredictable schedules, hospitality workers navigating tips and variable pay, and freelancers or gig workers who need to treat their income like a business.
